Grandma's Lemon Meringue Pie

One 9-inch pie
30 Min - Prep time
10 Min - Cook time
40 Min - Total
8 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 tbsp tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 3 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 2 lemons, juiced and zested
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 4 egg yolks, beaten
  • 1 (9 inch) pie crust, baked
  • 4 egg whites
  • 6 tbsp white sugar

Directions

  1. Preheat oven: Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Make the Filling: In a medium saucepan...
    • combine sugar, flour, and cornstarch. Add salt and water, and stir until smooth.
    • Cook over medium-high heat, stirring frequently, until mixture comes to a boil.
    • Stir in lemon juice and lemon zest.
    • Remove from heat, and stir in butter.
    • Gradually whisk in beaten egg yolks.
    • Return to heat, and bring to a gentle boil, stirring constantly. Cook for 2 minutes more.
    • Remove from heat, and pour into baked pie crust.
  3. Make the Meringue:
    • In a large glass or metal bowl, whip egg whites until foamy.
    • Add sugar gradually, and continue to whip until stiff peaks form.
    • Spread meringue over pie, sealing the edges at the crust.
  4. Bake in preheated oven for 10 minutes, or until meringue is golden brown.
